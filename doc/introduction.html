What is notifyme?¶

Notifyme is a python library that helps you transport and manage realtime notifications securely. It also comes with exemplary implementations for most of the components that are ready-to-use.

Meet the core components¶

Emitter
Generates notifications and forwards it to the collector.
Collector
The collector aggregates notifications from emitters and forwards them to a publisher
Publisher
The Publisher either generates notifications itself or distributes notifications that the collector aggregates to connectet subscribers.
Subscriber
A subscriber is a program that listens to notifications and reacts to them, i.e. presents it to the user.

Security¶

All components communicate with each other mutually authenticated and encrypted. This ensures that a user does not get forged notifications and that notifications cannot be read if the network between the com- ponents is being monitored or even compromised.
